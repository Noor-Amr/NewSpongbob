This is a sub-page of SpongeBob SquarePants: Battle for Bikini Bottom (GameCube, PlayStation 2, Xbox).

There are many unused audio clips in this game, ranging from entirely unused dialogue to early recordings of used dialogue, as well as several unused sound effects.
